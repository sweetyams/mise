# NYT Cooking Greatest Hits (Curated)

## 1. Buttermilk-Brined Roast Chicken (Samin Nosrat)

### Ingredients

-   1 whole chicken (3--4 lb)
-   2 cups buttermilk
-   2 tbsp kosher salt
-   1 tbsp black pepper \### Instructions

1.  Coat chicken in buttermilk, salt, pepper. Refrigerate 24 hours.
2.  Preheat oven to 425°F.
3.  Roast 45--55 minutes until skin is deeply golden and juices run
    clear.
4.  Rest 10 minutes before carving.

## 2. Momofuku Bo Ssam (David Chang)

### Ingredients

-   1 pork shoulder (8--10 lb)
-   1 cup sugar
-   1 cup salt
-   1/2 cup brown sugar \### Instructions

1.  Mix salt and sugar, rub pork. Chill overnight.
2.  Slow roast at 300°F for 6 hours.
3.  Finish at 500°F for 10 minutes to caramelise crust.
4.  Serve with lettuce wraps and sauces of choice.

## 3. No-Knead Bread

### Ingredients

-   3 cups flour
-   1/4 tsp instant yeast
-   1 1/2 tsp salt
-   1 1/2 cups water \### Instructions

1.  Mix into shaggy dough. Rest 12--18 hours.
2.  Bake in covered Dutch oven at 450°F for 30 minutes.
3.  Uncover and bake 10--15 minutes more until crisp and browned.

## 4. Lemony Shrimp & Bean Stew

### Ingredients

-   1 lb shrimp, peeled
-   2 cans white beans, drained
-   1 lemon, juiced and zested
-   3 garlic cloves, sliced
-   Olive oil, salt, pepper \### Instructions

1.  Sweat garlic in olive oil.
2.  Add beans and stock, simmer 15 minutes.
3.  Add shrimp, cook 3--5 minutes.
4.  Finish with lemon, season well, serve.

## 5. Ricotta Dumplings

### Ingredients

-   1 1/2 cups ricotta
-   1 cup parmesan, grated
-   2 eggs
-   3/4 cup flour
-   Salt, pepper \### Instructions

1.  Mix, form small dumplings.
2.  Boil until they float (about 2 minutes).
3.  Toss in butter or tomato sauce, serve immediately.

## 6. Peruvian Roasted Chicken with Spicy Cilantro Sauce

### Ingredients

-   1 whole chicken
-   2 tbsp cumin
-   2 tbsp paprika
-   1 cup cilantro
-   1/2 cup mayo
-   1 garlic clove
-   1 lime \### Instructions

1.  Season chicken, roast 425°F for 50 minutes.
2.  Blend cilantro, mayo, garlic, lime, salt, pepper.
3.  Drizzle sauce over carved chicken and serve.

## 7. Labneh Dip with Sizzled Scallions & Chile

### Ingredients

-   2 cups labneh or Greek yogurt
-   4 scallions, sliced
-   1 tbsp chili flakes
-   3 tbsp olive oil \### Instructions

1.  Spread labneh on plate.
2.  Heat olive oil, add scallions and chili, sizzle 1 minute.
3.  Pour over labneh, finish with salt and oil drizzle.

## 8. Shakshuka with Feta

### Ingredients

-   1 red onion
-   3 garlic cloves
-   1 can crushed tomatoes
-   1 tsp cumin
-   6 eggs
-   1/2 cup feta \### Instructions

1.  Sauté onion and garlic in oil.
2.  Add cumin and tomatoes, simmer 15 minutes.
3.  Make wells, crack eggs, cook covered 5--7 minutes.
4.  Top with feta and pepper.

## 9. Alton Brown's Shrimp in Green Sauce

### Ingredients

-   1 lb shrimp
-   1 cup parsley
-   1/4 cup olive oil
-   2 garlic cloves
-   1 lemon \### Instructions

1.  Blend parsley, oil, garlic, lemon, salt.
2.  Simmer sauce, add shrimp, cook 3 minutes.
3.  Serve over rice or toast.

## 10. Pot Roast (NYT Classic)

### Ingredients

-   4 lb chuck roast
-   3 carrots
-   3 celery ribs
-   2 cups red wine
-   2 cups beef stock
-   3 thyme sprigs \### Instructions

1.  Brown roast.
2.  Add veg, wine, stock, thyme.
3.  Braise 325°F for 3 hours.
4.  Strain sauce, reduce, serve sliced.

# Matty Matheson Top 5 Recipes

## Butter Chicken (Matty's Style)

### Ingredients

-   2 lb boneless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into bite-size pieces
-   2 tbsp ghee (or olive oil)
-   1 large onion, roughly chopped
-   6 garlic cloves, peeled
-   2 tbsp fresh ginger, peeled and chopped
-   2 tbsp tomato paste
-   1 can (28 oz) tomato purée or crushed tomatoes
-   1 tsp turmeric
-   1 tsp cumin
-   1 tsp coriander
-   1 tsp chili powder
-   2 tsp garam masala
-   1 tbsp dried fenugreek leaves
-   1 cup heavy cream (35%)
-   4 tbsp unsalted butter
-   2 tsp salt (adjust to taste)
-   Fresh cilantro for garnish (optional)

### Instructions

1.  Purée onion, garlic, ginger until smooth.
2.  Heat ghee in a large pot over medium heat.
3.  Add purée, cook 5 minutes.
4.  Add tomato paste and spices, cook 3 minutes.
5.  Add tomato purée and fenugreek, simmer 1 hour.
6.  Add chicken, cook 15 minutes.
7.  Stir in cream and butter, serve with rice or naan.

## Fluffy Pancakes

### Ingredients

-   2 cups all-purpose flour
-   3 tbsp sugar
-   1 tsp baking powder
-   1 tsp baking soda
-   1/2 tsp salt
-   2 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
-   4 eggs, separated
-   2 cups buttermilk
-   Blueberries (optional)
-   Maple syrup for serving

### Instructions

1.  Mix dry ingredients.
2.  Whip egg whites until fluffy.
3.  Mix yolks and butter, fold into whites.
4.  Fold in dry mix, rest 10 minutes.
5.  Cook on skillet until bubbles form, flip once.
6.  Serve with syrup.

## Spaghetti Pomodoro

### Ingredients

-   1 lb spaghetti
-   3 tbsp olive oil
-   5 garlic cloves, crushed
-   1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
-   1/2 cup fresh basil
-   2 tsp salt
-   1 tsp pepper
-   Olive oil for finishing

### Instructions

1.  Boil pasta until al dente.
2.  Cook garlic in oil 2 minutes.
3.  Add tomatoes, simmer 25 minutes.
4.  Add basil, toss with pasta.
5.  Finish with olive oil and basil, serve.

## Pot Roast

### Ingredients

-   4 lb chuck or rump roast
-   3 tbsp canola oil
-   2 tsp salt
-   2 tsp pepper
-   3 shallots, halved
-   4 carrots, large cut
-   3 celery ribs, large cut
-   2 leeks, rinsed and halved
-   2 tbsp Dijon mustard
-   2 tbsp tomato paste
-   2 cups red wine
-   2 cups beef stock
-   3 thyme sprigs
-   3 tbsp unsalted butter
-   1 tbsp lemon juice

### Instructions

1.  Brown seasoned roast in oil.
2.  Sauté vegetables 5 minutes.
3.  Add mustard and paste, stir.
4.  Add wine, stock, thyme, return roast.
5.  Cook covered in oven 325°F for 3 hours.
6.  Strain sauce, whisk butter and lemon, serve sliced.

## Beef Tourtière

### Ingredients

-   2 pie crusts (double pastry)
-   2 tbsp butter
-   1 tbsp canola oil
-   2 onions, diced
-   2 celery ribs, diced
-   1 leek, thin sliced
-   1 lb ground beef
-   1/4 tsp cinnamon
-   1/4 tsp cloves
-   1/4 tsp cardamom
-   1/4 tsp nutmeg
-   2 tbsp flour
-   1/2 tsp salt
-   1/2 tsp pepper
-   1 egg for wash
-   Ketchup for serving

### Instructions

1.  Brown aromatics and beef.
2.  Add spices, thicken with flour, cool.
3.  Fill pie, seal crust, vent top.
4.  Brush egg wash, bake 375°F for 45 minutes.
5.  Rest 10 minutes, slice, serve with ketchup.

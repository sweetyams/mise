# Brad Leone Top 5 Recipes

## 1. Stuffed Peppers (Brad Style)

### Ingredients

-   4 big bell peppers
-   1 lb ground beef (or turkey if you're feelin' fancy)
-   1 cup cooked rice
-   1 onion, diced
-   3 garlic cloves, minced
-   1 cup tomato sauce
-   1/2 cup shredded cheese
-   Salt, pepper, chili flakes \### Steps

1.  Slice tops off peppers, yank seeds out.
2.  Cook onions + garlic in oil.
3.  Add meat, spice it up, stir in rice + sauce.
4.  Stuff peppers like it's Thanksgiving.
5.  Top with cheese, bake 375°F for 40 min.

## 2. Garlic‑Miso Butter Mashed Potatoes

### Ingredients

-   2 lb potatoes
-   3 tbsp butter
-   1 tbsp white miso paste
-   2 garlic cloves
-   Milk, salt, pepper \### Steps

1.  Boil potatoes in salty water.
2.  Melt butter, mix in miso + garlic.
3.  Mash, pour milk, season, smash.

## 3. Brad's Spoon Sauce

### Ingredients

-   1/4 cup mayo
-   1/4 cup yogurt
-   1 tbsp mustard
-   1 tbsp honey
-   Lemon juice, garlic, herbs, salt, pepper \### Steps

1.  Mix everything in a bowl.
2.  Drizzle it on grilled stuff.

## 4. Cooler Corn

### Ingredients

-   6 ears of corn
-   1/4 cup butter
-   1 lime
-   Chili flakes, salt \### Steps

1.  Grill corn till charred.
2.  Toss in butter + lime + spice.

## 5. Crepe‑Pasta Manicotti

### Ingredients

-   1 cup flour
-   1 cup milk
-   3 eggs
-   Ricotta, parmesan, basil, sauce \### Steps

1.  Make crepes.
2.  Fill with cheese mix.
3.  Roll it, sauce it, bake it.

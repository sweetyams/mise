## Couscous with Cherry Tomatoes & Herbs

### Ingredients

-   1 1/2 cups couscous
-   2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
-   1/4 cup olive oil
-   1 small red onion, finely sliced
-   1/2 cup parsley, chopped
-   1/2 cup cilantro, chopped
-   1/3 cup mint, chopped
-   1 lemon, zested and juiced
-   1 garlic clove, crushed
-   1/2 tsp cumin
-   1/4 tsp chili flakes
-   1 tsp sea salt
-   Black pepper, to taste

### Method

1.  Pour couscous into a large bowl, add a pinch of salt.
2.  Bring 2 cups of water to a boil and pour over the couscous. Cover
    and let steam for 10 minutes.
3.  Fluff gently with a fork, allowing grains to separate like warm
    sand.
4.  Heat olive oil in a pan and add onion. Cook until softened, about 3
    minutes.
5.  Add cherry tomatoes, garlic, cumin, chili flakes, salt, and pepper.
    Cook 2--3 minutes until fragrant and glossy.
6.  Fold tomato mixture into couscous.
7.  Stir in herbs, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Adjust seasoning.
8.  Serve slightly warm or at room temperature, drizzled with olive oil.

## Za'atar Roast Chicken with Green Tahini Sauce

### Ingredients

#### Chicken

-   1 whole chicken (3--4 lb)
-   3 tbsp olive oil
-   2 tbsp za'atar
-   1 lemon, sliced
-   4 garlic cloves, crushed
-   1 1/2 tsp sea salt
-   Black pepper, to taste

#### Green Tahini

-   1/2 cup tahini
-   1/2 cup parsley
-   1/2 cup cilantro
-   1 small jalapeño, seeded
-   1 lemon, juiced
-   1 garlic clove
-   1/4 cup cold water
-   1/2 tsp salt
-   Black pepper

### Method

1.  Preheat oven to 425°F.
2.  Pat chicken dry and rub with olive oil, salt, pepper, and crushed
    garlic.
3.  Massage za'atar into the skin.
4.  Stuff cavity with lemon slices.
5.  Roast for 45--55 minutes until golden and juices run clear.
6.  Blend tahini, herbs, garlic, jalapeño, lemon juice, water, salt, and
    pepper until smooth and green.
7.  Drizzle generously over chicken before serving.

## Eggplant with Buttermilk Sauce

### Ingredients

-   2 medium eggplants, halved lengthwise
-   1/4 cup olive oil
-   1 1/2 tsp sea salt
-   Black pepper
-   1/2 cup buttermilk
-   1/3 cup Greek yogurt
-   1 garlic clove, crushed
-   1 lemon, juiced
-   2 tbsp olive oil
-   2 tbsp pomegranate seeds
-   1 tbsp parsley, chopped
-   1 tbsp toasted pine nuts

### Method

1.  Preheat oven to 425°F.
2.  Score eggplant flesh, brush with olive oil, season with salt and
    pepper.
3.  Roast 30--40 minutes until soft and caramelized.
4.  Whisk buttermilk, yogurt, garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and
    pepper until silky.
5.  Spoon sauce over eggplant, sprinkle with pomegranate, pine nuts,
    parsley, and seeds.

## Orzo with Prawns, Tomato & Marinated Feta

### Ingredients

-   1 lb orzo
-   3 tbsp olive oil
-   1 small red onion, diced
-   3 garlic cloves, crushed
-   1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
-   1/2 tsp chili flakes
-   1 tsp sea salt
-   1/2 tsp black pepper
-   1 lb prawns, peeled
-   1/2 cup feta, crumbled
-   1/4 cup basil, torn
-   1 lemon, zested and juiced

### Method

1.  Cook orzo in salted water until al dente. Drain, reserve 1/4 cup
    pasta water.
2.  Sauté onion in olive oil 3 minutes.
3.  Add garlic, chili, salt, pepper. Cook 1 minute.
4.  Add tomatoes and reserved pasta water. Simmer 10 minutes.
5.  Add prawns, cook 4--5 minutes.
6.  Fold in feta, basil, lemon zest and juice.

## Shakshuka (Ottolenghi Style)

### Ingredients

-   3 tbsp olive oil
-   1 red bell pepper, sliced
-   1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
-   1 small red onion, sliced
-   3 garlic cloves, crushed
-   1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
-   1 tbsp tomato paste
-   1 1/2 tsp cumin
-   1 tsp smoked paprika
-   1/4 tsp chili flakes
-   1 1/2 tsp sea salt
-   Black pepper
-   6 eggs
-   1/3 cup parsley, chopped
-   1/4 cup cilantro, chopped

### Method

1.  Heat olive oil in a deep pan.
2.  Add peppers and onion. Cook 6--8 minutes.
3.  Add garlic and spices. Cook 1 minute.
4.  Stir in paste, tomatoes, paste. Simmer 15 minutes.
5.  Make small wells, crack eggs, cover, cook 5--8 minutes until just
    set.
6.  Finish with herbs, olive oil drizzle, pepper.
